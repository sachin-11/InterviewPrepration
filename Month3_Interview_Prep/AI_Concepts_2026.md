# 2026 में AI के नए Concepts

## 1. Agentic AI (एजेंटिक AI)
- **Autonomous Agents**: AI जो खुद decisions ले सकते हैं और tasks complete कर सकते हैं
- **Multi-Agent Systems**: Multiple AI agents जो मिलकर complex problems solve करते हैं
- **Tool Use & Planning**: AI tools use करके, plan बनाकर, और execute करके goals achieve करता है

## 2. Multimodal Foundation Models
- **Text + Image + Audio + Video**: Single model जो सभी modalities समझता है
- **Unified Representation**: सभी data types के लिए shared latent space
- **Cross-Modal Reasoning**: एक modality से दूसरी में reasoning करना

## 3. Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) 2.0
- **Agentic RAG**: Agents जो dynamically retrieve, reason, और synthesize करते हैं
- **Graph RAG**: Knowledge graphs use करके better retrieval
- **Self-RAG**: Self-reflection और self-correction capabilities

## 4. AI Reasoning & Planning
- **Chain-of-Thought (CoT) Scaling**: Longer, more complex reasoning chains
- **Tree-of-Thoughts / Graph-of-Thoughts**: Multiple reasoning paths explore करना
- **Neuro-Symbolic AI**: Neural networks + symbolic reasoning combine करना

## 5. Small Language Models (SLMs) & Edge AI
- **Phi-3, Gemma 2, Llama 3.2 1B/3B**: Efficient models जो edge devices पर चलते हैं
- **Quantization (4-bit, 2-bit)**: Memory footprint reduce करना
- **On-device AI**: Privacy, latency, offline capabilities

## 6. AI for Science (AI4Science)
- **AlphaFold 3**: Protein structure prediction
- **Materials Discovery**: New materials design करना
- **Climate Modeling**: Weather prediction, climate simulation
- **Drug Discovery**: Molecule generation, binding affinity prediction

## 7. Synthetic Data Generation
- **High-Quality Synthetic Data**: Real data जैसा synthetic data generate करना
- **Data Augmentation at Scale**: Training data की कमी दूर करना
- **Privacy-Preserving**: Real data के बिना training

## 8. AI Alignment & Safety
- **Constitutional AI**: Principles-based alignment
- **Scalable Oversight**: Weak supervisors से strong models align करना
- **Interpretability**: Mechanistic interpretability, sparse autoencoders
- **Red Teaming**: Automated adversarial testing

## 9. World Models / World Simulators
- **Video Generation as World Models**: Sora, Veo, Gen-3 - physics समझते हैं
- **Action-Conditioned Video Prediction**: Action के base पर future predict करना
- **Embodied AI**: Robots के लिए world understanding

## 10. Personalized AI / Memory-Augmented AI
- **Long-term Memory**: User preferences, history याद रखना
- **Adaptive Personalization**: Real-time adaptation to user
- **Privacy-First**: Local processing, federated learning

## 11. AI-Generated Code & Software Engineering
- **Autonomous Coding Agents**: Devin, SWE-agent - full software lifecycle
- **Code Generation + Testing + Debugging**: End-to-end development
- **Repository-Level Understanding**: Large codebases समझना

## 12. Energy-Efficient AI
- **Neuromorphic Computing**: Brain-inspired hardware
- **Analog AI Chips**: Lower power inference
- **Sparse Training**: Mixture of Experts (MoE), activation sparsity
